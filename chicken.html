Butter Chicken with Garlic Naan

Enjoy a classic Indian dish with a creamy, flavorful twist!

Ingredients

For Butter Chicken:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

For Garlic Naan:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Butter for brushing
  • Fresh chopped coriander for garnish

Instructions

Butter Chicken:

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Marinate for at least 1 hour.

  2. Heat butter in a pan. Add marinated chicken and cook until golden brown.

  3. Add tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes. Add cream, adjust salt, and cook until the sauce thickens.

  4. Garnish with fresh coriander. Set aside.

Garlic Naan:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and yogurt. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Divide dough into small balls, roll out each ball, and sprinkle with minced garlic and coriander.

  3. Cook each naan on a hot skillet until golden and slightly charred, then brush with butter.
